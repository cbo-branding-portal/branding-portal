  Branding Conversations in FY22
CBO Branding Conversations is one of the key activities under Special Recruitment Initiatives with the objective of optimizing candidate-organization relationship from the pre-hiring stage itself. The initiative has been devised in order to understand the queries of yet to join candidates and improve their understanding of the firm processes. The respective Talent Group leaders reach out to the candidates and connect with them to understand if any interventions are required. These feedbacks are passed on to the Talent team for any further action if required.
